Food World

Healthy Meals

Healthy meals are made with fresh ingredients such as vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.

They provide important nutrients that help the body stay strong and energized.

Fast Food

Fast food is quick to prepare and popular with many people around the world.

Common fast foods include burgers, pizza, fries, and sandwiches.

Desserts

Desserts are sweet foods enjoyed after meals or during special occasions.

Popular desserts include cakes, ice cream, chocolate, and fruit pastries.
